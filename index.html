About

I’m John Oni, a multi-skilled digital professional blending creative tech with operational precision. I specialize in front-end web development, CRM workflows, email marketing, and virtual business support, helping brands and teams build systems that look good, work fast, and scale smoothly.

My work spans coding clean, responsive websites, optimizing customer engagement processes, and delivering hands-on support across digital channels. I also create content around digital skills and freelancing on my YouTube channel, The Digital Hustler.

With a growing background in health and technology, including hands-on lab experience and exposure to digital tools in healthcare, I bring a unique perspective to tech projects with real-world impact. Whether it’s code, content, or customer systems, I build with clarity and craft.
